About DKeith Wilson

Unique Value Proposition: change leader, team builder, continuous internal innovator that knows his way around a balance sheet and has the leadership, communication, and technical chops to get things done. I’ve continuously sought out new challenges over the last 15+ years- never afraid to leap from my comfort zone and begin anew.

What makes me unique? In short, I’m a weird confluence of seemly opposing skills and traits. I think strategically, yet execute tactically. I’m passionate, though oddly analytical. I’m a tech guy that can sell and manage the financials. And most importantly, I’m a leader that believes in service to others.

My professional (and personal) philosophy can be summed up in what I call the 4 P’s (People, Passion, Purpose, and Push): passionate people with a shared purpose can push down any obstacle- it’s really that simple.
